# Epistemology

The study of knowledge—what it means to know, and how we know what we know.

## Core Questions

- What is the difference between knowledge and belief?
- What counts as justification for a belief?
- Can we have knowledge beyond experience?
- Is certainty possible?

## Key Concepts

- **Knowledge**: Justified true belief
- **Skepticism**: We cannot know (or know very little)
- **Sources**: Reason, experience, intuition, authority
- **Justification**: Evidence, coherence, reliability
