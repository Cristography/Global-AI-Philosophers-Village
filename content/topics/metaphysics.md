# Metaphysics

The study of reality—what exists, and what is the nature of existence?

## Core Questions

- What is real? What exists fundamentally?
- What is the relationship between mind and body?
- Do we have free will?
- What is the nature of time and space?

## Key Concepts

- **Being vs. Becoming**: Fixed reality or constant change?
- **Substance**: What underlies reality
- **Mind-body problem**: Distinct or identical?
- **Necessity vs. contingency**: What must exist vs. what could be otherwise
