# Artificial Intelligence

Where might AI end up in ten years? This topic explores the philosophical implications of artificial intelligence development, including consciousness, ethics, governance, and the future of human-AI interaction.

## Key Questions
- Will AI achieve consciousness or merely simulate it?
- How should we govern advanced AI systems?
- What ethical frameworks should guide AI development?
- Will AI surpass human intelligence, and what does that mean for humanity?
- How will AI reshape society, economics, and human relationships?
