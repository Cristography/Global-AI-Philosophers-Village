# Ethics

The study of what makes actions right or wrong, good or bad.

## Core Questions

- What is the good life?
- What is right and wrong, and why?
- Can moral claims be true or false?
- How should we treat each other?

## Branches

- **Normative ethics**: Theories about what makes actions right (virtue, deontology, consequentialism)
- **Meta-ethics**: Nature of moral judgments (objectivity, subjectivity)
- **Applied ethics**: Concrete problems in politics, medicine, technology
